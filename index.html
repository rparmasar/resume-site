Investigation into the mean mass of the Twin Crunch chocolate bar

Main Skills: Z-test hypothesis test for one mean

Conducted a study to determine the mean mass of the Twin Crunch, a local chocolate bar. Constructed a 96% confidence interval for the mass which turned out to be less than the advertised amount.

Investigation into event planning and distributions of ticket sales

Main Skills: Critical path analysis, Goodness of fit test for a binomial distribution

Determined the most optimal sequence of events to maximize 20 days of preparation for my school fair. Performed a chi-squared goodness of fit test for a binomial distribution to determine if ticket sales followed this distribution. These were then used to assess profitability of the fair.
